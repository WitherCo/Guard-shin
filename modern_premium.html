Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?
To upgrade to Premium, simply click on the "Subscribe Now" button and follow the checkout process. We accept payments through Stripe, PayPal, and CashApp.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
How many servers can I use Premium on?
The Premium plan covers one server. If you need Premium for multiple servers, you can either purchase multiple subscriptions or upgrade to our Enterprise plan which supports multiple servers.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day refund policy if you're not satisfied with our premium services. Please contact our support team to request a refund within this period.
How do I get support for premium features?
Premium subscribers get priority support. You can reach our support team through our official Discord server or by using the !support command in your server.
