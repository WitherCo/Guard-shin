Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

To upgrade to Premium, simply select your preferred plan and click the "Get Started" button. You'll be redirected to our secure payment page where you can complete your purchase. Once the payment is processed, your server will be automatically upgraded with Premium features.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. When upgrading, you'll only pay the difference for the remaining time on your current billing cycle. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

How many servers can I use Premium on?

The Basic and Standard plans can be used on one server. The Premium plan can be used on up to 3 servers. If you need more servers, please contact us about our Enterprise plan for custom pricing and unlimited server usage.

What payment methods do you accept?

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our premium features. After this period, we do not provide refunds for the current billing cycle, but you can cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges.

Who can I contact for support?

For any questions or support needs, you can join our support server or contact us via email at support@guardshin.com. Premium subscribers receive priority support with faster response times.
