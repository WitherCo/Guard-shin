Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium by clicking the "Subscribe" button on any plan above. You'll be redirected to our PayPal payment page. After completing your payment, join our support server to have your premium features activated within minutes.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your billing cycle.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the new plan will take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do you offer a free trial?

We don't currently offer a free trial for premium plans, but you can use the free version of Guard-shin to try out basic features before upgrading.

How do I get help with premium features?

If you need help with premium features, you can join our support server or contact us directly through the support page.
