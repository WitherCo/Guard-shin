Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate Premium after subscribing?

After subscribing, you'll need to link your Discord account through our dashboard. Then you can select which server(s) to apply Premium to. Your Premium status will be active within minutes of completing payment.

Can I transfer Premium to a different server?

Yes, you can change which server(s) receive your Premium benefits at any time through our dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, allowing you to transfer Premium status as your needs change.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept payments through Stripe (credit/debit cards), PayPal, and CashApp. For CashApp payments ($kingsweets2004) and PayPal (ChristopherThomas429), please message us in our support server after payment so we can verify and activate your subscription manually.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for monthly subscriptions if you're not satisfied with the Premium features. Lifetime plans can be refunded within 30 days. Please contact our support team to request a refund.

Do you offer discounts for multiple servers?

Yes! Our Premium+ plan allows you to apply Premium features to up to 3 servers. For larger communities or organizations needing more than 3 Premium servers, please contact us for custom pricing options.

Can I get Premium without paying?

Yes, there are two ways to get Premium without a direct payment:

  1. Boost our support server (gives you Premium for 1 server)
  2. Participate in our partnership program (contact us for details)
