Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate premium features?

After purchasing a premium subscription, premium features will be automatically activated for your server(s). You'll receive a confirmation in your server's system channel, and premium commands will be immediately available for use.

Can I transfer my premium subscription to another server?

Yes! Premium subscriptions can be transferred between servers that you own. Use the !premium transfer command in the new server after removing the bot from the old server. Note that you can only perform a transfer once every 30 days.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for premium subscriptions if you're not satisfied with our services. For refund requests, please contact our support team through our Discord support server or via the support page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards through Stripe, PayPal, and CashApp. All payments are secure and encrypted. For special payment arrangements or bulk purchases, please contact our sales team.

Do I need to pay for each server?

Premium subscriptions are per server. However, our Enterprise plan allows management of multiple servers. We also offer bulk discounts for communities that manage multiple servers - please contact our sales team for details.
