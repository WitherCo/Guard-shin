Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium by selecting one of our subscription plans above and following the payment process. Once your payment is processed, your server will instantly get access to all premium features.

Can I transfer my Premium subscription to another server?

Yes, you can transfer your Premium subscription to another server at any time through the dashboard. Simply navigate to your subscription settings and select the new server you want to apply Premium to.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your server will revert to the free tier with standard features.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new Premium subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with the Premium features, contact our support team within 7 days of your purchase for a full refund.

Is there a discount for non-profit or educational Discord servers?

Yes, we offer special pricing for verified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our support team with verification of your status to receive information about our discount program.
